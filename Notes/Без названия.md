**Reconstruction loss** — token-level cross-entropy between decoder logits and the true peptide.

**KL loss** — divergence between the approximate posterior produced by the encoder and a standard Gaussian prior.

**Posterior collapse** — the main technical risk
Solution steps:
- KL warm-up
- Free bits (KL clipping)
